最后两天可背模板

Recently, more and more people have begun to realize the importance of XX. In a world full of changes and challenges, XX is no longer a minor choice, but an essential ability/quality for personal growth and future development. Therefore, it is necessary for us to understand why XX matters so much.

First, XX helps people deal with problems more effectively. For college students, it can improve learning efficiency, broaden their horizons and enable them to make wiser decisions. For example, students who attach importance to XX are more likely to stay focused, overcome difficulties and make steady progress in their study and life. Second, XX also plays a key role in long-term development. It not only builds confidence and independence, but also prepares people for future competition in society. Without XX, one may easily fall behind in a rapidly changing world.

In conclusion, XX is closely related to individual development and social progress. It is high time that we paid more attention to it and took practical action to develop this valuable ability/quality. Only in this way can we better face future challenges and create a more promising future.

逐句中文意思

  1. 最近，越来越多的人开始意识到 XX 的重要性。
  2. 在一个充满变化和挑战的世界里，XX 不再是一个次要选择，而是个人成长和未来发展所必需的一种能力/品质。
  3. 首先，XX 能帮助人们更有效地处理问题。
  4. 对大学生来说，它能提高学习效率，拓宽视野，并帮助他们做出更明智的决定。
  5. 例如，重视 XX 的学生更可能保持专注、克服困难，并在学习和生活中稳步进步。
  6. 其次，XX 对长期发展也起着关键作用。
  7. 它不仅能建立自信和独立性，也能帮助人们为未来的社会竞争做好准备。
  8. 总之，XX 与个人发展和社会进步密切相关。
  9. 我们是时候更加重视它，并采取实际行动去培养这种有价值的能力/品质了。
  10. 只有这样，我们才能更好地面对未来挑战，创造更有希望的未来。

替换词包

技术/风险题微调

When used properly, XX can bring great convenience to our study, work and daily life.

However, if people depend on it too much or use it irresponsibly, it may waste their time, weaken their judgment and even cause unnecessary problems.

Therefore, we should make full use of its advantages while avoiding its possible risks.

先背这 12 个六级翻译骨架

位于/坐落于

XX is located in YY.

XX 位于 YY。
有...历史

XX has a history of over 000 years.

XX 有 000 多年的历史。
被认为是

XX is regarded as one of the most important YY in China.

XX 被认为是中国最重要的 YY 之一。
由...组成

XX consists of A, B and C.

XX 由 A、B、C 组成。
覆盖/涉及

XX covers a wide range of fields, including A and B.

XX 涉及很多领域，包括 A 和 B。
发挥重要作用

XX plays an important role in YY.

XX 在 YY 中发挥重要作用。
促进发展

XX has greatly promoted the development of YY.

XX 极大促进了 YY 的发展。
带来好处

XX has brought great benefits to local people.

XX 给当地人民带来了巨大好处。
自...以来

Since 0000, XX has made great progress.

自 0000 年以来，XX 取得了巨大进步。
不仅...而且...

XX not only shows YY, but also reflects ZZ.

XX 不仅展示 YY，也反映 ZZ。
越来越...

In recent years, XX has become increasingly important/popular.

近年来，XX 变得越来越重要/受欢迎。
为...作贡献

XX has made great contributions to YY.

XX 为 YY 作出了巨大贡献。

翻译考场步骤

  1. 先找主语和谓语，句子太长就拆成两句。
  2. 不会的文化词可以拼音加解释，比如 hutong, a traditional alley。
  3. 年份、地点、历史、作用优先写清楚，不要为了高级句硬凑从句。
  4. 每段至少保证主谓完整：不要只写名词短语。
  5. 翻完后重点查单复数、时态、介词和拼写。

常见主题词

不会词时这样保命

  1. 专有名词题目给英文就照抄；没给就用拼音 + 简单解释，比如 chengyu, a Chinese idiom。
  2. 不会高级词就用简单上义词：工程 project，设施 facility，建设 construction，贡献 contribution。
  3. 长中文句先拆：背景一句 + 主干一句 + 结果一句。少写大长从句。
  4. 数据句优先保证数字和单位：kilometers, cubic meters, billion yuan, years。
